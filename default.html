Info

You can search for sounds or use your local files. All searched sounds use the CC0 license.

If you want to save a searched sound, drag it to a slot in My sounds. You can change the slot by dragging.

If you want to delete a sound, drag it into the trash can.

You can play and pause a sound by pressing its key on your keyboard. You can change these keys by double-clicking the button and pressing the new key. All numbers and letters are allowed. If the key is already in use, the keys will switch places.

You can move between My sounds pages by clicking or pressing the left and right arrow keys on your keyboard.

You have no more space in My sounds.

Please delete some sounds if you wish to upload more files.
